A GoPro is a compact, durable, and waterproof action camera designed for capturing high-quality video and photos in extreme conditions. Known for its wide-angle lens and versatility, it’s widely used in sports, adventure, travel, and vlogging. GoPros can be mounted on helmets, vehicles, or gear, making them popular for first-person point-of-view (POV) shots. They also feature advanced stabilization, time-lapse, slow motion, and wireless connectivity.

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing, sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It features fast, technologically advanced cars racing on circuits and city streets around the world. The F1 season consists of a series of races called Grands Prix, where teams and drivers compete for championship points. Known for speed, precision, and innovation, F1 blends elite engineering with intense global competition, drawing millions of fans worldwide.
