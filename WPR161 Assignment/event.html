Red Bull Presents: The Ultimate Go-Kart Showdown

“Built to Race. Powered by You.”

Get ready to rev your engines because Red Bull is bringing you an event like no other — the Red Bull Kart-Off! This isn’t just any race… this is where creativity meets adrenaline.

This year, Red Bull challenges YOU to bring your custom-built go-kart to the track and compete for glory, speed, and some jaw-dropping prizes.

What’s The Catch? Build It. Race It. Win It.

The Red Bull Kart-Off is an open-invite event for all go-kart fanatics, backyard engineers, speed junkies, and creative racers. But there’s one rule — you’ve gotta bring your own go-kart.

→ Not just any go-kart though...

Your kart must meet the official universal standards for competitive karting:

  • Safe Design (no harmful spikes, sharp edges, or damaging features)
  • Weight Limits (light enough for speed, but heavy enough for stability)
  • Size Limits (standard kart dimensions apply)
  • Speed Capabilities (within karting regulations)

What’s Up for Grabs? The Grand Prize is Insane.

The winner of the Red Bull Kart-Off walks away with more than just bragging rights:

  • VIP Formula 1 Tickets to any Red Bull Racing GP of your choice
  • A Year's Supply of Red Bull featuring our 4 New Flavours:
  • Exclusive Red Bull Merch Pack
  • Including limited-edition gear you won't find in stores
